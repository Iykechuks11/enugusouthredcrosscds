Hope for Humanity

The Nigerian youth corp members Red Cross Society is a subsidiary of the Nigerian Red Cross Society (NRCS). The NYSC (Enugu-South) red cross community developers, is recognised by the Federal ans state government as a Voluntary Aid Society, auxiliary to the public authorities. This shows that we are a creation of the Federal Government of Nigerian and statutorily meant to complement public authorities in the area of humanitarian interventions.

Our Mission

The NYSC Red Cross prevents and alleviates human suffering in the face of emergencies by mobilizing the power of volunteers. We also go to schools and sensitize the children and adults on how to use of the first aid box.

Volunteer

We, the NYSC Red Cross Community developers devote our time and talents to make real difference in the lives of little children here in Enugu south. Our duties are to serve as volunteers when the need arises and also provide first aid care and training to every school in Enugu south.

Training

We are taught the latest and best emergency science. This trainings does not only teach us steps for perfoming first ais, but equips us with the confidence and skills necessary to perfoem them correctly.

Sensitizations

We visit schools and religious houses to educate both young and old on some life-saving techniqes and skills they can employ when their is an emergency in their commuinity, surroundings and home.
